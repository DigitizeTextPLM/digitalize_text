She couldn’t remember what they had been talking about or which ‘lark’ she would
have to stop, only that significant tiny word shouted into her very soul a horn of
warning, and with it was coupled a disembodied fragment of information, which now
took on an ominous significance: Nigel had been seen in Farlingham on an occasion he
had sworn he had been standing in for Luke Parsons at his surgery in Little Phelpham.
