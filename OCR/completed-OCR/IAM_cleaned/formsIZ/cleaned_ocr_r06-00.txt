Vence is a sober spot, half way between small town and village, pigeon grey, sly with
arches, and linked by a whispering plot of fountains. In the main tree-heavy square
you can sit in the autumn sunshine, still burning like a half-cooled iron, sip pastis and
read the local newspapers.
