But £4000 for 1000 Prisoners is the same proportion as £4 for 1 Prisoner: it follows therefore that according to the above computation, £4 will be the premium for which such intended Governor will be to be made Creditor on account of any individual Prisoner on the day which delivered to his charge, on condition of his being made Debtor to the amount of £100 in the event of such Prisoner's dying in his custody within the compass of a year reckoning from the said day: and so in proportion for any term less than a year Moreover in case of any dispute touching the amount of such premium or the payment of the said eventual head--money of £100 according to the rate of calculation herein above fixed, the same shall be settled by arbitration as above.
