Prince Souvanna Phouma has not yet replied to the mission, but recent statementsmade by him in Phnom Penh indicate that he still regards himself as the only legalPrime Minister of Laos. His policy of strict neutrality from 1951 to 1958 kept thekingdom in peace, though at the cost of virtual partition of the country into thepro-communist north and the pro-western centre and south.
