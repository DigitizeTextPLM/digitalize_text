The party was now preparing to see the tapestries, and in the general movement,Vittoria was separated from Orsini. Although neither of them wished to follow thesightseers, there seemed no alternative. As she was about to mount a wide andshallow flight of marble stairs, she became aware of someone watching her intently,and turning in that direction, she saw Olimpia, standing beside her admirer, OrlandoCavalcanti.
