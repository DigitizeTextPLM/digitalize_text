Compared with 1958, the expenditure index for 1959 showed increases of 4 to 6 percent for couples without children and those with one child, and much smaller changesfor couples with several children. The quantity index, which has risen only slowlysince 1956, was almost unchanged in 1959 for couples with two or more children, butrose by 3 per cent in the older two-adult households and by 1-2 per cent for youngerchildless couples and couples with one child.
