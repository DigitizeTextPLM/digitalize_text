Their friendship for Augusta became rather hollow, and the news that Byron had lefther practically all his money caused it to crumble to oblivion. Though Lady Byronknew perfectly well that Byron, as early as the year of their wedding, 1815, had madea will in Augusta's favour, she had evidently not passed on that information; and itcame as an appalling surprise to Captain Byron that he had been left without thefortune that would keep up the title.
