JOHN DICKIE writes: Mr. Macmillan gave top priority to the clash over NorthernRhodesia on his return from Scotland yesterday. He summoned Mr. Iain Macleod,Colonial Secretary, and Mr. Duncan Sandys, Commonwealth Relations Secretary foran hour's talks at Admiralty House. A statement is expected today to hold the dooropen for modifications to the new Constitution provided law and order is maintainedin Northern Rhodesia.
