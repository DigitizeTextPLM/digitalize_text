By the end of the month he still delighted in Naples. He told Cloncurry that heenjoyed it as much as his health permitted him to enjoy anything. 'The Pearl', hewrote, 'is arrived, which is a great resource. Vesuvius seems to be tired; he is goingout fast.... What a gay, lively people, and what a busy town. At Rome, every otherman was a priest: here the priest is 1superceded by the soldier - a favourable changein my eye, particularly as the troops are very fine.'
