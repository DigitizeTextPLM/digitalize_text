To Beryl, at least, this was anti-climax with a vengeance. She had expected to be metwith violent reproaches - and here he was bland and smiling. But it did not take herlong to realise that his mood was less pleasant than appeared on the surface. He toldher nonchalantly, as he led the way to his own veranda, that he had taken the libertyof asking Jack - as a representative of the Vallin family - to come over and make thepresentation in her stead.
