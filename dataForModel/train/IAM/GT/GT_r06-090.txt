It was growing cold. We left the crowd and drove back to Vence. The cool eveningperfumes stood beckoning at the corners of the roads. Mart is unable to smell (hersense organs were impaired years ago), and I had to explain the low, sharp and sweetsignals in the air. When we got back home we felt exhausted. London sickness (asense of guilt, mingled with the memory of sandwiches and incestuous Soho pubs) stillnumbed our brains and bodies.
