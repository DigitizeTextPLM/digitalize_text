The "pot-au-feu" is as much a national institution in France as is tea drinking amongourselves and it is prepared at least once a week in every bourgeois household. Thack-eray, of course, waxed enthusiastic about Bouillabaisse and sang - This Bouillabaissea noble dish is, A sort of soup, or broth, or brew, A hotch-potch of all sorts of fishesThat Greenwich never could outdo; Green herbs, red peppers, mussels, saffron, Soles,onions, garlic, roach and dace.
