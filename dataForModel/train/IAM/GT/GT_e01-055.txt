One can remember with horror the pictures of the overcrowded and over-embellisheddrawing-rooms complete with heavy crochet antimacassars, mantelpiece covers witha fringing of clumsy bobbles and numerous other crocheted pieces. It was some yearsafter the Victorian period that designers realised the potentialities of the craft, andcrochet was revived with designs suitable for contemporary trends. Crochet todayhas a variety of uses, and has even stepped into the field of high fashion.
