Navigator Sid is a tower of strength when newly-weds Ian Carmichael and JanetteScott let loose their ancient houseboat on a honeymoon trip down the river. The filmstarts off brightly enough but, half-way through, the plot (as well as the boat) springsa near-disastrous leak. Familiar members of Britain's repertory team of comedycharacter-actors jump through equally familiar hoops to mild laughter.
