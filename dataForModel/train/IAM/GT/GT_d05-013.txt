They objected to the word Absolution. So the phrase 'or the remission of sins' wasadded. But Absolution remained. They objected to the word Confirmation. So thephrase 'or the laying on of hands upon children baptized and able to render an accountof their faith according to the Catechism following' were added. But Confirmationremained. Other changes in the Book were equally minor except for the new sectionon the Sacraments added to the Catechism.
