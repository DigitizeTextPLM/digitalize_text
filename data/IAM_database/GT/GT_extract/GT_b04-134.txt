For expansion today means still greater prosperity tomorrow. BERTRAND RUS-SELL, the 88-year-old standard bearer of the Ban-the-Bomb crusade, has a devotedfollowing. Thousands march with him - and sit with him too. It is said by some thathe is a saint; by others that he is a prophet. He is, in fact, a philosopher with a highlydeveloped sense of publicity who has been spectacularly wrong on the great issues ofour time.
