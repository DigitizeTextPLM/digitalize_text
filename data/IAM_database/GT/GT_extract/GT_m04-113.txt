Dai, meanwhile, was pedalling furiously on the road. Not a sign of the bus. Hepassed The Traveller's Joy. At last he caught up Mr. Roberts, walking very doggedtowards Llangrwl. &quot;Afternoon, Mr. Roberts,&quot; he called, polite, as he shot past. Hisspirits were rising. He had seen no sign of an accident. Therefore Mr. Roberts musthave been mistaken. He would find the bus safe and sound in Llangrwl, and his dearMorfydd quietly having tea in her own home.
