The average gross return on established industrial estates ranges from 7 to 9 per centof the capital expenditure on land, site works and buildings. Manufacturing industryaffords employment to large numbers of non-manual as well as manual workers. Ananalysis made by Crawley Development Corporation in 1958, after collating repliesfrom fifty-eight manufacturing firms employing over nine thousand staff, showed that5 per cent of the staff were classed as managerial and administrative, 11 per cent astechnical or employed in research and 19 per cent as clerical.
