They mostly occurred in lonely farm-houses. Monsieur H, for instance, had beenclubbed and throttled to death by his wife, children and father-in-law, after muddlingup some sheep while the worse for drink. The family group then sat down for a latelunch before the father-in-law telephoned the police. Then again, Monsieur V, owingto family troubles, had written to the local paper and the superintendent of police,informing them that he was on the point of committing suicide, and gratefully leavinghis house appurtenances and utensils to the superintendent.
