HOW the Government must repent its haste and folly in Rhodesia! Eighteen monthsago this territory was peaceful, orderly, and thriving. Africans within the Federal Gov-ernment were getting valuable experience in administration. Then Mr. Iain Macleodbecame Colonial Secretary. Suddenly everything changed. Timetables were scrapped.The ill-conceived Monckton Commission was rushed out to Rhodesia. Overnight, mi-nor African politicians were inflated into international figures.
