Vittoria, accompanied by her maid, Lucia, was on her way to the Accoramboni villa,ostensibly to pay a daughterly call on her father, actually to coax money from thatindulgent parent to settle her mounting debts. Lucia was thinking how beautifulher mistress looked, and how cunningly the olive-green dress with its underskirt ofrose-brocade fitted her perfect figure. Vittoria's thoughts were more complex.
