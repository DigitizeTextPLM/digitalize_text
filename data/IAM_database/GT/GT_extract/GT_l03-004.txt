The blow took the writhing thug a fraction above the boney knob which landmarkedthe cervical plexus, the vital nerve-centre which a long-ago Marine instructor haddeclared the pinnacle of unarmed combat targets. The thug collapsed with a whistlingmoan, and Kennan tore the Luger from the man's suddenly limp hand. There was notime for rejoicing, but the hard, firm shape of the automatic sent a new confidencesurging through his body.
