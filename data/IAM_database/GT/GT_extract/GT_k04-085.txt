This reverie was rudely ended as the coach gave a sickening jolt and came to anabrupt stop, nearly throwing the two girls from their seats. Lucia uttered a cry ofalarm. 'Look, madonna, we are surrounded!' It was true. Men armed with pikesand daggers swarmed about them and a lean, swarthy fellow was peering through thewindow, grinning impudently. 'Santa Maria!' shrieked Lucia. 'Banditti!'
