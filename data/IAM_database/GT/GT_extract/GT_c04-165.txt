The film replaced &quot;What's My Line?&quot; and &quot;Be My Guest&quot; programmes because ofan electricians' strike. It showed Britain today through the eyes of an American TV reporter, Eric Sevareid, and British personalities. Among them - Professor DennisBrogan, Shelagh Delaney, and Alan Sillitoe. The film covered a wide aspect of theBritish scene, ranging from pubs, the Eton wall game, to the European CommonMarket.
