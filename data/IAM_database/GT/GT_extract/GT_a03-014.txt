Two emissaries from the Laotian Government of Prince Boun Oum have arrived inPhnom Penh, Cambodia, to invite Prince Souvanna Phouma, the former Prime Min-ister, to return to Laos. No details of their mission have been disclosed, but it wasreported earlier in Laos that Prince Boun Oum was considering asking Prince Sou-vanna Phouma to join his Government.
