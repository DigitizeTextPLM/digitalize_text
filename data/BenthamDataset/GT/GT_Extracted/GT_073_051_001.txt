In treating of this Title the following is the Order which I shall pursue 1st. I shall establish a Definition of the Crime. 2nd I shall exhibit the several exemptive Distinctions, that have been taken out of this Definition by the positive Establishment of our Common Law, to which I shall give the Title of Anomalities restrictive. 3dly. The various correctives that have been administer'd to them respectively by the Hand of Legislature. These I shall call Corrections to those Anomalities. 4thly.
